Privacy Policy

Saint Thomas Ventures LLC (Ridge Co)  |  Effective Date: January 1, 2025  |  Last Updated: June 2025

1. Who We Are

Saint Thomas Ventures LLC, operating as Ridge Co, is a property maintenance coordination company based in Baltimore, Maryland. We coordinate maintenance services between property owners, tenants, and contractors.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information solely for the purpose of coordinating property maintenance services:

— Contact information (name, phone number, email address) for tenants, property owners, and contractors.

— Property information (address, unit number, maintenance history) necessary to perform services.

— Maintenance request details submitted by tenants or property owners.

3. How We Use Your Information

Information is used exclusively to coordinate and communicate about property maintenance services. This includes sending SMS notifications regarding work order assignments, scheduling, and status updates.

We do not use your information for marketing purposes. We do not send unsolicited messages.

4. SMS Communications

By providing your phone number to Ridge Co or your property owner, you consent to receive SMS messages related to maintenance coordination. These messages may include work order notifications, scheduling confirmations, and status updates.

Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not share your information for marketing purposes.

Contact information may be shared between tenants and contractors solely for the purpose of scheduling and completing a maintenance job you have requested or approved.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. Access to sensitive property and contact data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

7. Data Retention

We retain contact and maintenance records for the duration of our service relationship and for a reasonable period thereafter for record-keeping purposes.

8. Your Rights

You may request that we update or remove your contact information at any time by contacting us directly.
